What's inside

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built by someone who's been through the job hunt grind. Every feature exists for a reason.

📋
Kanban Job Tracker

Move applications through Applied → Phone Screen → Interview → Offer → Rejected. See your entire pipeline at a glance and never lose track.

📄
Resume Builder

Build a clean resume with live preview. Customize fonts, colors, spacing, and templates. Download a fully ATS-readable PDF every time.

💌
Cover Letter Generator

Structured editor with live preview. Generate a tailored letter for any saved job with one click using Gemini AI.

🔗
AI Job Extraction

Paste any job posting URL — AI automatically extracts the company, role, and requirements. No more manual copy-pasting.

🎯
Resume Match Score

Get a 0–100 score showing how well your resume matches each job. Know your strongest applications before you hit send.

✍️
AI Bullet Tailor

Paste a job description and AI rewrites your experience bullets to highlight the skills and keywords the employer is looking for.
